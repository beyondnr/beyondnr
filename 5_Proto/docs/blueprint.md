## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#A0D2EB) to convey trust and stability, aligning with the legal compliance aspect.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F7FA) to provide a clean and uncluttered workspace.
- Accent color: Muted green (#86C8BC) to highlight important actions and success states.
- Headline font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif for headlines or body text.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif font for the main text.
- Simple and clear icons to represent different actions and data points within the application.
- Mobile-first design with a focus on large, easily tappable buttons and clear visual hierarchy.
